Atmospheric noise from the edge of the web

Yeo. I’m Rekrypt — an electronic artist and producer based in Calgary, AB. I make music that’s vivid, disorienting, and immersive. Warping through genres like Acid, Bass, and Breaks, I lean into gritty basslines and esoteric melodies to craft soundscapes that feel experimental but intentional.

My work is rooted in a love of late-century digital media, underground bass culture, and emotional experience. It comes out as dark, percussive, defective rhythms— stuff that makes you want to glitch out on the dancefloor.

Beyond sound, I design and program custom visuals for my sets and streams—layered, glitch-heavy, and stark. I draw from chiaroscuro lighting and fragmented digital textures, inspired by Ryoji Ikeda, Hiromasa Ogura, David Lynch, and Albrecht Dürer.
