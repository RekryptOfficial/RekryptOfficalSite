About Rekrypt.

Rekrypt. is an electronic artist and producer based in Calgary, AB. Their work revolves around themes of technology, bass, esotericism, glitch aesthetics, and chiaroscuro art.

They design custom visuals that are glitch-heavy and stark, inspired by Ryoji Ikeda, Hiromasa Ogura, David Lynch, and Albrecht Dürer.

For collaborations, Rekrypt. works with Modern Grief.
